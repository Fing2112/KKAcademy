Our Creed

Built on Honor, Tempered by Fire

We did not build a gym. We built an order.

Founded by lifelong martial artists who believed the modern world had forgotten what training was truly for, Indomitus exists to forge complete people — not just better fighters. Every class is a small act of self-mastery. Every belt, a record of perseverance. Every member, a guardian of the standard.

We hold the line on discipline because the world will not. We demand excellence because you are capable of it. And we do it together, because no one becomes indomitable alone.

Head Coach · MMA

A national MMA champion with 18 years on the mat, Maximus founded Indomitus on a single principle: relentless standards build relentless people. He leads the competition team and head-coaches the advanced program.
