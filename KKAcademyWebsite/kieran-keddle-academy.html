Meet the Founder

Learn From One of the Best to Do It

Kieran Keddle is a former 3-time world Muay Thai champion who held the UK No.1 ranking for eight straight years and made history as the first British fighter to compete at both Lumpinee and Rajadamnern Stadiums in Bangkok. Across 78 professional fights he built a reputation for elite striking and durability on stages from Thailand to Japan to Morocco.

Today he's ranked among the world's top 10 striking coaches, has developed 23 world champions, trained Idris Elba for the Discovery series Fighter, and founded Canada's leading pro Muay Thai promotion. Now he leads KK Academy across Alberta — bringing world-class striking to your doorstep.

The Corner Team

Our Coaches

Decades of combined experience across striking, grappling and the fight game — in your corner from day one.

Founder · Head Coach

Kieran Keddle

3-time world Muay Thai champion and UK No.1 for eight years — the first Brit to fight at Lumpinee and Rajadamnern.

Across 78 pro fights he competed worldwide before becoming a top-10 striking coach globally, developing 23 world champions and training Idris Elba on Discovery's Fighter. Founder of the Muay Thai World Cup, Canada's leading pro promotion.

Coach · Striking & MMA

Barry McDonald

A lifetime in martial arts across boxing, Muay Thai, boxing, judo, BJJ, wrestling and MMA.

Barry has developed world, North American and national champions, and trained everyone from police and military to pro fighters and total beginners. His philosophy: everyone's welcome, no ego, no excuses — just real results.

Coach · MMA & Striking

Wes Kemp

Over 20 years in martial arts with a BJJ foundation and a well-rounded boxing, boxing and MMA game.

A core member of Canada's Shaved Bears Pro Team from 2015–2022, Wes trained daily alongside elite athletes and is known for technical precision, high fight IQ and developing strikers through detailed pad work.

Coach · Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Gary Ward

A BJJ purple belt with over 10 years on the mats, focused on a welcoming, beginner-friendly room.

Training under Professor Tim Blanchard (Alavanca), Gary builds strong fundamentals at every student's own pace. His favourite moment? Watching a technique finally click during sparring.

Coach · Multidiscipline

Abraham Kilian

Nearly two decades coaching, representing Team Canada in boxing and karate, and a BJJ brown belt champion.

A Master's in Law and training in sport psychology let Abrie coach beyond technique. Between 2021–2024 he developed 13 Canadian champions, including a world No.2 in BJJ — building resilient, focused athletes on and off the mat.
